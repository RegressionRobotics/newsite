About Team Regression

Regression Robotics, known as FTC Team 30432, is a nonprofit dedicated to advancing robotics engineering. With a team size of 7 members, we compete in the FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC), focusing on designing, building, and programming innovative robots. Based in Naperville, IL, our name 'Regression' reflects our commitment to analyzing data and refining strategies to achieve excellence.

As part of FIRST, we embrace teamwork, gracious professionalism, and STEM education. Our team leverages tools like Java, Android Studio, and advanced techniques such as machine learning and regression analysis to optimize our robot's performance, inspired by the FTC community's innovative spirit. Visit us at www.regressionftc.com.

Join us as we compete, learn, and grow in the exciting world of robotics!
